# AIM Cockpit Manager

The companion app for your Invictus cockpit. It connects your panels, stick, and
displays to your flight simulator and keeps everything working while you fly.

## What it does

- **Finds your gear automatically** and walks you through setup — no manual configuration files.
- **Makes every switch, knob, and button work** in **DCS World** and **Falcon BMS**.
- **Puts your displays on extra monitors** — MFDs, DED, RWR, HUD, and more.
- **Keeps your hardware in sync with the sim** in real time.
- **Updates itself** — and your hardware — automatically.

## Download

Grab the latest installer from the [**Releases**](../../releases) page and run it.
Once installed, the app updates itself whenever a new version is available.

## Requirements

- Windows 10 or 11
- DCS World and/or Falcon BMS
- Invictus AIM cockpit hardware
